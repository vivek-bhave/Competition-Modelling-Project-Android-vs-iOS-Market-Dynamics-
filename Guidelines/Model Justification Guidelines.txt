Model Justification Guidelines

Goal of this stage:

We must justify why we use a nonlinear competition model instead of other models.
Before fitting the model, we need to understand what kinds of models are used to study markets, technology adoption, and competition.

We will explore different types of models used in economics and technology studies.

Task 1 — Search different types of models

Look for papers using:

* regression / statistical models
* time series / forecasting
* diffusion models (Bass model etc.)
* game theory
* agent-based models
* differential equation / Lotka–Volterra / logistic models

Task 2 — For each paper, write summary

Use format:

Paper title:
Year:
Problem studied:
Model used:
Data used:
Main result:
Limitation:
Future work:
Relation to our project:

Task 3 — Compare models

For each model, think:

* Can it describe competition?
* Can it show saturation?
* Can it show stability?
* Can it show long-term behavior?
* Can parameters be interpreted?

Task 4 — Justify our model

Explain why nonlinear competition model is suitable:

Possible reasons:

* system has limited total market
* two systems compete
* growth is nonlinear
* behavior changes over time
* need equilibrium and stability analysis

Task 5 — Write conclusion

Write short conclusion:

* what models exist
* what their limitations are
* why nonlinear dynamical model is needed
* why our model is suitable for this data

Output of this stage:

* paper summaries
* comparison of models
* written justification of chosen model
