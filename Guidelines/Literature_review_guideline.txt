We are starting the Literature Review stage of our modelling project.

Before moving to the next stages, we need to understand what work has already been done in this area.
This will help us justify our model, understand limitations of previous studies, and explain why our work is needed.

Each person should search for research papers related to:

* competition models
* Lotka–Volterra / nonlinear models
* technology / market share / diffusion
* smartphone / platform competition
* prediction / forecasting models
* recent papers (preferably after 2018)

While reading papers, try to answer these questions:

1. What different models are used in economics / technology competition?
2. What exactly did the paper study?
3. What are the limitations of that work?
4. What future work did the authors suggest?
5. How does our project overlap with that work?
6. Why is our model or study still needed?

For each paper, write the summary in the following format:

Paper title:
Year:
Problem studied:
Model used:
Data used:
Main result:
Limitation:
Future work:
Relation to our project:

Important rules:

* Do not copy abstract directly
* Write in your own words
* Focus on understanding the model and idea
* Prefer recent papers (last 5–6 years if possible)
* Each person should find 1–2 good papers

Goal of this stage:

After literature review, we should be able to explain:

* what has already been done
* what is missing
* why our work is needed
* why we chose this model
